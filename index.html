Donald "Smitty" Schmidt

March 28th, 1935 - October 13, 2021

Donald, or as I knew him..."Pops!". The MAN...The LEGEND!!!
This page is dedicated in honor of the great Donald Schmidt! We all love and miss you.

He was one of my biggest influences on my life and taught me just about everything I know.

  • March, 28th 1935 - Born in New York
  • December 2nd, 1955 - Joined Navy
  • December 30th, 1956 - Married Barabra Slater
  • September 11th, 1957 to 1961 - Joined Reserves
  • 1967 - Moved his family to Central Florida
  • Served in Kodiak Alaska as a aircraft engine mechanic
  • Had 6 siblings - 2 sisters and 4 brothers
  • Had 4 children, 9 grandchildren, and 9 great grandchildren
  • October 13th, 2021 - Died
  • October 2nd, 1990 - Met his favorite grandson Zach Wolf (Me)
